Algorithm:

We follow a simple algorithm that lies on principle that Nifty falls 7% to 10% less than its all time High 3 to 4 times a year and in long run Nifty always increases.

  • We wait till Nifty is 10% below its all time high value.
  • When Nifty is 10% below its previous high, we become sharks and invest in top companies who are also down by 10% or atleast as much as Nifty and who generally bounce back when nifty bounces back
  • We book profits as soon as we are at or close to 52 week high values
  • We repeat the steps 3-4 times in whole year

We must note that this follows when the companies are down not because of their fundamentals but because of the market movements. Also this stratergy is inspired by Mindyourinvestment and you can look at the whole reasoning on their Youtube channel.
